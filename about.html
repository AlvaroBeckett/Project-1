Howzit? This application was created by agroup of developers who are passionate about finding the right bar at the right time! Howzit is an application that allows a user to locate bars and brewerys in their area! Some key features that all users love is the ability to rate the current status of the establishment, and update the girl/guy ratio. Like what you see when you browse? Howzit has an integration with Uber to get you to your destination safely. Logon on and see Howzit going!
